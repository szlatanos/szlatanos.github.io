Welcome to my page!

I am a PhD student at King’s Business School with a doctoral research studentship from the Qatar Centre for Global Banking & Finance. My supervisors are Prof. George Kapetanios and Prof. Francesca Monti.

Prior to embarking on a PhD, I worked for two years in the Business Cycle Analysis Division of the Directorate General Economics of the European Central Bank.
